Fluffy Homemade Pancakes

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Eggs 1 large
Milk 3/4 cup
Vinegar 2 tbsp
Melted Butter 2 tbsp
Baking Powder 2 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Cinnamon 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Combine milk and vinegar and set aside.
Step 2: Sift flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon together.
Step 3: Add milk, eggs and butter to flour mixture and stir just until combined. Do not over mix.
Step 4: Heat lightly greased skillet. pour batter into pan to form a 4-5 inch disc. Flip pancake when sides begin to turn solid. Pancake is done when both sides are golden brown.
Step 5: Serve warm pancakes with syrup or jam.

Creamy Broccli and Chicken Pasta

  • Time:1 hr
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Penne Pasta 2 cups
Broccoli Florrets 2 cups
Chicken Breasts 2 Pieces
Milk 1/2 cup
Cheddar cheese 1 cups
butter 2 tbsp
flour 2 tbsp
Salt and Black Pepper 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Bring 8 cups water to a boil and add pasta. Cook until aldente.
Step 2: Steam Broccoli for 5 minutes. Colour will be bright green. Immediately transfer to ice bath to stop cooking. Set aside.
Step 3: Asd 2 tbsp olive oil to a large skillet. Add Seasoned chicken breast and pan seer until golden brown on each side. Set aside to cool then cut into cubes.
Step 4: In the same skillet add the butter. When melted add flour and combine to make a soft paste. Allow to cook for 1 minute while stiring.
Step 5: Remove from heat and add milk. Stir constantly until all lumps are desolved. Return to heat and stir until mixture begins to thicken. Season with salt and pepper to taste
Step 6: Add cheese and stir until melted. Add pasta and stir to coat in sauce. Add broccoli florets and cubed chicken and stir to combine. Serve hot.

Saltfish Cakes

  • Time: 1 hr
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Saltfish 1 lb
Flour 1 cup
Water 1/2 cup
Green Seasoning 1 lb
Egg 1
Baking Powder 1 tsp
Cooking Oil 1 cup

Step 1: Soak salt fish in boiling water for 30 minutes then drain. Taste for appropriate salt content. Repeat if necessary until desired taste.
Step 2: Shred saltfish into tiny pieces and combine with all other ingredients except oil.
Step 3: Heat oil in skillet. Drop spoon full of batter into hot oil. Flip over when fish cake begins to float. Cook until golden brown on all sides.
